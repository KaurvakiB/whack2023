Mission Statement

Moving to a new environment can be tough for students where you may be far from home, from family, and friends. As college students ourselves, we understand the struggles and tolls life in school can take on one's mental state. We wanted to provide a resource for you all to use to raise awareness about your own mental health, but also to find sources you can potentially use!

Think of us as your College Companion!
